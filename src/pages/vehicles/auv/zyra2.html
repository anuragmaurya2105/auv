Zyra 2.0

Our latest Autonomous Underwater Vehicle, prepared for the RoboSub 2022 competition. The vehicle marks a significant improvement on our previous vehicles since it is our latest vehicle equipped with a functioning acoustics system. The end product is a cost-effective, robust and small design capable of underwater gripping, dropping, torpedo launch, acoustic localization and advanced image recognition algorithms.
Vehicle's Dimensions

Length: 0.61 m

Breadth: 0.51 m

Width: 0.35 m

Embedded Systems (Acoustics)

Raspberry Pi 3B+

Custom designed DAQ

Sonar

BlueView Subsea M750d Dual-Frequency Multibeam Sonar

Mass of Vehicle

24 kg in Air

Propulsion System

BlueRobotics T200 Thrusters (x7)

Manipulators

Custom designed electrically actuated torpedo launcher

Top Speed

1.5 m/s

Navigation Components

Teledyne RDI Pathfinder DVL

Sparton AHRS-8 IMU

BlueRobotics Pressure/Depth Sensor

Fizoptika FOG

Power System

14.8V 16000mAh LiPo Battery (x2)

Degree Of Freedom

6 (Surge, Sway, Heave, Roll, Pitch, Yaw)

Vision Equipments

FLIR BFS-PGE314SC-C Camera (Forward Facing)

FLIR BFS-PGE314SC-C Camera (Downwards Facing)

Software Architecture

Ubuntu 18.04 LTS
